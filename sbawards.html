IEEE Hyderabad Section - Student Branch Awards
Call for Nominations

Introduction


      IEEE Hyderabad Section recognizes the role of Student Branch (SB) in representing and fulfilling the needs of the members and the mission of Hyderabad Section, R10 and IEEE. The IEEE Student Branch Award is given to a SB that has maintained good membership, volunteering opportunities, and helped in the promotion of IEEE vision and mission. These awards help to improve the membership development, satisfaction, and retention.


The Student Branch Awards are given to recognize the contribution made by SBs for a period from 1st November 2018 to 31st July 2019.




Awards


  1. Lead Student Branch Award - to recognize and encourage the SBs that continued to be active after reception of the vibrant award and continuously participating in SB exhibits yearly
  2. Vibrant Student Branch Award - to recognize and encourage SBs above three years old that fulfilled IEEE mission and vision by organizing admirable activities
  3. Emerging Student Branch Award - to recognize and encourage SBs established in recent three years for their notable activities


Purpose


  The purpose of these awards is to identify SBs that has conducted quality events, workshops, projects, etc. for the benefit of students.



Scope


  One award under every category for every year. However the final decision is taken by the jury members.



Eligibility


  • Any IEEE SB within Hyderabad Section is eligible to contest for these awards
  • Outstanding initiatives and performance in organizing activities
  • The SB should not be the recipient of any of the above awards in the past two years
  • The SB must have organized at least six activities in the given time frame


Judging Criteria


The student branch exhibits will be evaluated by the panel of judges on following criteria:-

  • Regularity of events conduction at their SBs
  • Outreach of events to the student members
  • SB’s Membership growth and retention rate
  • Effective presentation at the exhibits
  • Sustenance of IEEE Chapters/ Affinity groups if any etc

In precise, the SB that lives up to the motto of adhering to the IEEE's mission of "Advancing Technology for Humanity" will be awarded.



Award Presentation


  The awards will be announced in the valedictory of SSC '19 and the certificates will be presented during the Annual General body Meeting (AGM) of IEEE Hyderabad Section.



Schedule


Call for Nominations: 27th July 2019


Submission Deadline: 05th August 2019


Exhibits Date: 10th August 2019




Guidelines

  1. Participation in the exhibits is through nomination only.
  2. Student Branch can showcase their branch activities using charts, photographs or any other presentation material (restricted to maximum FOUR A3 size posters).
  3. Student branches are recommended to carry their own resources and tools required for displaying their exhibits.
  4. Presentations through projectors are strictly not allowed.
  5. Any disturbance or discrepancies caused regarding the space allotted for stalls at the exhibition area will not be encouraged.
  6. It is mandatory for the students representing their student branch at the exhibition area to carry their IEEE membership card and college ID otherwise they are not permitted inside.
  7. Only 4 IEEE members from a student branch are allowed at the exhibition area to help in the process of arranging the stall.
  8. Only 2 executive committee members from a student branch are permitted to present the exhibit (Student Network Members and Branch Counselors are not encouraged during the assessment of exhibits).
  9. The Exhibitors must maintain a good decorum at the exhibit area and also with the judges.
  10. SBs are obligated to present only the events that were conducted during the specified time period.
  11. Ensure L31 reporting is up to date at the time of submission of nomination.
  12. Reports of all the events showcased by the SB should be uploaded in the Google form.
  13. Appealing for rejudgment is strictly not encouraged.
  14. The event organizers at any stage of the competition can disqualify the teams if the teams are found offensive.
  15. The presenters have to follow IEEE Code of Ethics.

APPLY HERE

 IEEE Hyderabad Section is one of the most active sections of IEEE in India. IEEE was formally introduced in Hyderabad, in June 1981, when a group of engineers in Hyderabad decided to form an IEEE sub-Section here. This sub-Section was rapidly elevated to a full Section, in 1984 (the year of IEEE Centenary celebrations)
